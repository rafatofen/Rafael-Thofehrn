Saboarte Natural

Branding & Social Media

Conception of the branding, packaging, brochures and social media for Saboarte Natural - all natural and vegan cosmetics.

Visit Instagram

Challenge

Create the brand of Saboarte Natural - handmade all natural and vegan cosmetics, its packaging, brochures and social media.

Solution

Hand drawn the logo, chose delicate typography and nature related colors to fit with the idea of natural and vegan cosmetics, the packages were designed to be printed on recycled paper, all social media photos have been meticulously arranged in natural settings to help convey the brand's core image.
