Saboarte Natural

Full branding — packaging, brochures and social media — for Saboarte Natural, a handmade all-natural and vegan cosmetics brand.

Solution

Hand-drew the logo, chose delicate typography and nature-inspired colours to align with the all-natural, vegan proposition, then extended the system to packaging and social content.
